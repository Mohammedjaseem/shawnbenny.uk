The cost of digital marketing will also depend on the specific goals and objectives of the marketing campaign, as well as the target audience and the level of competition. There are many different tactics and techniques that can be used in digital marketing, including search engine optimization (SEO), content marketing, social media marketing, email marketing, pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, and affiliate marketing.

Digital marketing is needed because it allows businesses to reach and engage with consumers where they spend much of their time: online. Digital marketing helps businesses to target specific groups of customers and track the effectiveness of their marketing efforts.
